Our Farming Services

Goat Farming

Training and resources for healthy goat rearing, promoting sustainable livestock practices.

Chicken Farming

Support for smallholders to raise poultry efficiently with proper feeding and health care.

Pig Farming

Guidance on hygiene, feeding, and breeding to improve pork quality and market access.

Catfish Farming

Support in aquaculture, pond management, feeding, and disease prevention.

Plantain Farming

Eco-friendly plantation support with propagation, fertilization, and harvesting guidance.

Corn Farming

Training in soil preparation, pest control, and irrigation for increased yield.

Cassava Farming

Best practices for cassava planting, stem selection, and harvesting for profitability.

Vegetable Farming

Fresh produce support with training on organic vegetable cultivation, soil nutrition, and pest control.

Beekeeping

Promoting eco-friendly beekeeping practices for honey production and crop pollination.
